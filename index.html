Frequently asked questions

What's Interact?

Interact is a service club for young people ages 12-18 who are passionate about making a difference while learning about the world and themselves. And having fun!

What's Rotaract?

Rotaract is a global service organization for young adults (18-30), sponsored by Rotary International. It fosters leadership, professional growth, and community service while promoting international understanding.

What is Rotary?

Rotary is a global humanitarian organization of business and professional leaders dedicated to service, peace, and community development. Founded in 1905, Rotarians focus on health, education, and poverty alleviation while fostering leadership and international fellowship.
